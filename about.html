PROFILE

I have worked at A-insinöörit Oy in the Nuclear waste management unit (NWM), and its predecessors Saanio & Riekkola Oy and B+Tech Oy in Finland as Designer and laboratory technician from April 2011– August 2020.
First, I have Knowledge and experience in quality control in a manufacturing environment, skills in analytical thinking, planning, and organizing. Experience in collaborating and working in teams, the capability to work in multitask in a rapid-paced condition.

I have Knowledge in experimental test set-ups, design of test equipment, installation and monitoring equipment, testing and sensors calibration, excellent command of MS Excel and MS office tools, experience in data collection and analysis, Tensile strength measurement, and reporting. Moreso, I gained experience in the preparation of various solutions in the chemistry lab according to the standard operation procedure (SOP), maintaining lab functionality, and managing lab organization to a standard.


Besides, I have experience in Software development Skills (Front-End) [HTML, CSS, React, and Sass], Python for data analysis, and Back-End with NodeJS. In addition, I gained experience in cost efficiency analysis on product and production.
I have a degree in Mechanical Engineering and Production Technology, which gives me experience in technical designs based on the conceptual drawings using SolidWorks software, convert 3D to 2D, and vice versa through SolidWorks. Recently, I completed a master's degree program in the field of Chemical Engineering with advanced specialization in Biobased process Engineering and Energy Technology as a minor.

Furthermore, I can work in any environment to contribute my idea, making positive changes. I portray attributes of hard-working, industrious, diligent, ready to obey the instruction, and easy to understand the instruction base on any specific task. I am looking forward to new challenges in ICT and Energy Technology.
